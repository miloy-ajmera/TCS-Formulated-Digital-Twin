Appearance

			Fine white powder			
Function

			Additive for candy,emulsifier, or surface active agent.  Produce materials for cosmetics, balsam, cold cream and hair oil etc..  Excellent emulsifier for unguent.  GMS is largely used in baking preparations to add "body" to the food. It is responsible for giving ice cream and whipped cream its smooth texture. It is sometimes used as an anti-staling agent in bread.			
Synonyms

			Glyceryl Stearate [GMS] Fine Powder Food Grade Kosher; GMS Fine Powder FCC USP Kosher; Distilled Monoglyceride ” Type P(V) Fine PDR K (GMS) “; Glycerol Monostearate; Unipeg GMS-P40			
Storage

			Keep container tightly closed. Keep in a dry, cool place.  Keep away from direct sunlight.
