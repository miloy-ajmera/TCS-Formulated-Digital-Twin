            Makeup & Eye Shadow Bases
        

Whether you are making your own make-up and lipstick for personal use, or you are making it to sell on a larger scale, our line of makeup and lipstick cosmetic base ingredients are made with the highest quality and are sure to set your product ahead of the curve.
All of our pigment powders are thoroughly blended with a machine blinder to achieve the ultrafine conformity needed to make a superior product. Each pigment provides a superior color saturation and purity that has outstanding results. Our pigments are great for basic makeup color for a multitude of makeup products including face powders, foundations, makeup creams, blush, bronzing powders, and more.
If you are interested in making lipsticks or lip balms, our selection of signature lipstick bases are the perfect starting point. From antioxidant to moisturizing, our lipstick bases are designed with superior quality. For a beautiful matte finish to your lipstick, check out our matte liquid lipstick base #1 and #2. Both are customizable with liquid red colorants and colored mica.
