Cosmowax™ J

Technical Datasheet
|

Supplied by Croda







Cetearyl alcohol (and) Steareth-20 (and) steareth-10. Cosmowax™ J by Croda acts as a non-ionic emulsifying wax. Used in skin care creams, lotions, sun care, baby creams, lotions, depilatories, antiperspirants/ deodorants and color cosmetics. Cosmowax™ J is also applicable for hair conditioners, hair styling, eye care, feet, hands, nails, hair bleaches, chemical relaxers/straighteners and hair colorants.
