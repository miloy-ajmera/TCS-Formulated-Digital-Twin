DYNASAN® 118

Technical Datasheet
|

Supplied by IOI Oleo







Tristearin. DYNASAN® 118 by IOI Oleo acts as a consistency agent for water-free, oil-based emulsions and products. It is triester of glycerin and stearic acid. It is 100 % natural, vegan and stable against oxidation. It finds application in cake/pressed powders as binders and in creams & lotions as body-imparting and structure-forming components. Furthermore, it is used as adjuvant in stick formulations and as crystallization accelerators & seeding agents to improve the solidification process. DYNASAN® 118 is used in color cosmetics, self-tanning formulas and skin-, baby-, body-, hair-, beard- and sun care products. It has a shelf life of 24 months. It is Ecocert and Cosmos approved.
