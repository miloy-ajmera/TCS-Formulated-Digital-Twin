Crodafos™ CES

Technical Datasheet
|

Supplied by Croda







Cetearyl Alcohol (and) Dicetyl phosphate (and) Ceteth-10 Phosphate. Crodafos™ CES by Croda acts as an o/w emulsifier and conditioning agent. Ideal for use in waterproof sun care products where it boosts SPF protection. It can also be used in hair relaxer systems to promote rapid penetration of hydroxide ions. It helps in preventing cuticle damage due to over processing. Crodafos™ CES is used in sun protection products, baby care, color cosmetics, hair conditioners, hair colorants, permanent waves and depilatories.
