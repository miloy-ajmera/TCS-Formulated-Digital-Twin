                            Super Hartolan™ pharma
                        












                        Super Hartolan™ pharma
                    












Excellent emollient with natural moisturising properties for topical emulsions and ointments. Nonionic W/O emulsifier and O/W emulsion stabiliser that is used in applications such as moisturising creams and protective baby creams. Recommended topical usage levels of 0.5-3%.
