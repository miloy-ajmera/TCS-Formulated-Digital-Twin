                        Super Hartolan™
                    












A natural lipid rich in sterols. Imparts moisturising, conditioning and protective benefits to both skin and hair. Highly effective W/O emulsifier and excellent emulsion stabiliser. Serves as a superfatting agent in soaps and detergent systems.
INCI name
Lanolin Alcohol

                    Water-in-Oil Cream (feat Super Hartolan)
                

This classic cream contains Super Hartolan™, a natural moisturizer and emollient that intensively nourishes and soothes the skin. The versatile Cithrol™ DPHS helps to stabilise this formulation and imparts a light and elegant skin feel.(JP0148)
