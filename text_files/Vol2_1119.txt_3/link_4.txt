Neutral massage oil (Vaseline) - 1 L

Neutral liquid oil is a dermatological emollient and protectant that is odourless and does not irritate the skin.
Used by physiotherapists and massage therapists to promote lubrication of the area to be treated. Contains no parabens, no allergens for even greater skin tolerance and compatibility.
Characteristics:
- Colourless oily product
- Neutral scent
- Relative density
- Easy to apply
- Massage oil with high sliding power and high moisturising power.

How to use:
Apply to healthy skin with a light massage to facilitate absorption. Formulated for rubbing and therapeutic or relaxing massages.
