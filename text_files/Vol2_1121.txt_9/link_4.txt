Our Whipped Cream fragrance is a deliciously light and creamy scent of fluffy white whipped cream, subtly enhanced with the sweetness of vanilla. his fragrance is phthalate free.
We use jojoba oil as the base oil for our perfume oils. Jojoba oil is very similar to the oil your skin produces. It absorbs quickly into your skin without leaving a greasy feeling. Roll on our perfume oil with our travel friendly glass bottles. This perfume oil bottle is small enough to meet airline regulations and fit into your purse along with being eco-friendly glass and recyclable plastic.
Ingredients: Simmondsia Chinensis (Organic Jojoba) Seed Oil, Fragrance.
