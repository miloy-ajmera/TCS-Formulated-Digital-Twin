The Kids Line Protective Cream by Officina Profumo-Farmaceutica Santa Maria Novella is specially formulated for the skin of the little ones. The formula is enriched with olive oil and shea butter to calm, soothe, and protect the skin of babies and children, especially in case of reddening and chapping.



The fragrance is characterized by a delicate fragrance with talc notes.

                          800 Years of Legacy
                        





Officina Profumo-Farmaceutica di Santa Maria Novella sets its roots back to 1221. Founded as a Dominican friars convent, it is renowned from its beginnings for the formulation of perfumes and remedies. Its apothecary art legacy crosses eight centuries and is deeply intertwined with Florence’s history: considered world’s oldest pharmacy, it carries its activities in the very places where they all began.












                      How to Use
                    





                    It is suitable for both face and body. Apply as needed with a light massage for skin that is protected and has a velvety feel.
