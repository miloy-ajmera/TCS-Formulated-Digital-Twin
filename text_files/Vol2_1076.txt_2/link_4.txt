AFRIDERM® EYE CREAM
Smooths & Defines Eye Contours
For All Shades of Dark Skin
 
30 ml (1 fl oz)
 
Take rapid action against dark circles and puffiness with Vitamin E & Botanical Oils
 

Swiftly boosts hydration with Potassium Lactate, imparting dark tones with renewed, soothing moisture.
A special blend of Botanical Oils, including Jojoba, Borage, and Aloe Vera, calms the eye contour, visibly reducing puffiness and crow’s feet.
Antioxidant Vitamin E works with a unique herbal complex to combat free radicals and defend against harmful environmental factors.
Mediatone® brings out skin’s naturally luminous glow, lightening and minimizing dark circles in the eye area.
The perfect pampering care to restore a rested, youthful look.

 
COMPLETE REPAIRING & PROTECTIVE CARE FOR YOUR DELICATE EYE CONTOUR
 
Ingredients: Demineralized Water, Glycerin, Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride, Isotridecyl Isononanoate, Cetearyl Glucoside, Galactoarabinan, Potassium Lactate, Mediatone, Algae Extract, Phenoxyethanol, Ethylhexylglycerin, Glyceryl Stearate, Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba) Seed Oil, Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea Butter), Aloe Barbadensis (Aloe) Leaf Juice, Tocopheyl Acetate (Vitamin E), Oenothera Biennis ( Evening Primrose) Oil, Borago Officinalis (Borage) Seed Oil, Fragrance, Hippophae Rhamnoides (Sea Buckthorn) Oil, Lilac Extract (Syringa Vulgaris), Tripeptide 10 & Citrulline, Ubiquinone, Linoleic Acid (Omega 6), Linolenic Acid (Omega 3), Green Tea.
 
Use: Apply a small amount to the cleansed eye area with the fingertips and massage in gently. For best skincare results, use in combination with AFRIDERM® Day Cream SPF-15, Serum Concentrate & Night Cream.
