DOWSIL™ 200 Fluid, 50 cst.

Technical Datasheet
|

Supplied by Dow







Dimethicone. DOWSIL™ 200 Fluid, 50 cst. by Dow is non-polar, non-reactive, substantive, dry and less oily dimethicone fluid. It acts as a carrier and conditioning agent. It offers low surface tension, broad viscosity range, water barrier, spreading and lubrication.
It provides de-soaping, de-tackification, slip and soft & smooth skin feel.
DOWSIL™ 200 Fluid, 50 cst. finds application in formulating antiperspirant/deodorant (aerosols, gels, sticks & creams), skin care (creams & lotions), sun care and cosmetic products.
