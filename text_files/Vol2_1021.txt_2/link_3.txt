Supplied by BASF







Ceteareth-25. CREMOPHOR® A25 by BASF is an oil-in-water emulsifier. It possesses absorbing, protective and stability increasing properties. CREMOPHOR® A25 is used in ointments, creams, liquid emulsions and gels.
