Glyceryl Stearate SE is an anionic self-emulsifying cream base. It is a mixture of mono-, di- and triglycerides, especially palmitic and stearic acid, blended with potassium stearate. It is used in cosmetic O/W emulsions for skin and hair care products. Glyceryl Stearate SE finds application in after-sun, body-, face & color care products, baby care & cleansing and face cleansing formulations. Also used in self-tanning, sun protection and conditioning & hair coloring products.
