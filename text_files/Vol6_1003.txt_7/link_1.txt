DESCRIPTION
-


This talc-free baby powder alternative goes on as a cream and transforms into a velvety finish to minimize chafing and irritation. With a clean, talc-free formula made from plant-derived ingredients, our baby powder alternative helps to nourish and moisturize those beautiful little rolls. This extra-gentle baby care cream is made with our proprietary sugarcane-derived squalane, a moisturizing superhero that baby's skin was born to love.



EWG Verified™



Leaping Bunny Certified



Pediatrician Approved 



Dermatologist Tested   








HOW TO USE
+


 Apply Baby Cream to Powder anywhere you’d put baby powder—i.e., where baby gets sticky, chafed, or sweaty. Gently smooth onto skin for a velvety finish.  





KEY INGREDIENTS
+







Squalane replenishes what they’re born with 
The vernix is baby's first built-in moisturizer—the best protection Mother Nature can give—and it’s rich in nourishing squalene. Through clean chemistry, we created a proprietary sugarcane-derived version of squalene, called squalane, that’s safe and sustainable—and it’s a moisturizing superhero that baby's skin already knows and loves.







Tapioca starch absorbs and wicks away wetness
This vegetable-derived, talc-free powder helps wick away excess wetness and minimize chafing while giving this baby powder alternative its silky-soft texture.







Mango butter supports the skin’s moisture barrier
Plant-derived, antioxidant-rich mango seed butter sinks in right away and helps bolster skin’s natural moisture barrier, the first line of defense against irritation.
