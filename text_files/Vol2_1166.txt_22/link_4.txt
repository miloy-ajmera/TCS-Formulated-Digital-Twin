ArmorCore

ArmorCore residential resilient sheet flooring is designed specifically for the unique needs of the builder and multi-family property owner. It provides a winning combination of resilience, aesthetics, durability and value. Offering the same designs and colors in three different constructions delivers style that provides visual continuity in quality products for every budget.
ArmorCore is a functional felt-back sheet flooring that offers tremendous value at a great price.

Dense and durable with excellent resistance to cuts and gouges.
Stylish contemporary designs
The most stain resistant wear surface of any sheet product at this price point
Meets HUD/FHA requirements
FloorScore Certified for clean indoor air quality
Proudly manufactured in the US
