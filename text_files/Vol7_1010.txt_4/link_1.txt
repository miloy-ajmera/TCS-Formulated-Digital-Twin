SOFTIGEN® 701

Technical Datasheet
|

Supplied by IOI Oleo







Glyceryl Ricinoleate. SOFTIGEN® 701 by IOI Oleo acts as a film former. It can be used in nearly all skin care preparations such as creams, lotions, bath oils, shaving formulations, refatting soap and shower formulations. SOFTIGEN® 701 is well tolerated by the skin and mucosa.
