Productdescription
Rich, Smooth, Moisturised
The Revitalising Eye Cream illuminates the eyes in a nourishing way. A finely tuned combination of active ingredients moisturises wrinkles caused by skin ageing. Exquisite organic plant extracts and fair trade organic oils firm and even out the tender area around the eyes with a warm fragrance and a fruity-floral note – without losing that charming smile around the eyes.
Barrier-strengthening Care – Ceramide Complex CLR™, Vitamin E and Apricot
Fine wrinkles around the eyes can be prevented with an innovative ceramide complex containing a blend of different phytolipids. These plant-based ceramide precursors made from phospholipids and sphingolipids balance the moisture content of the area around the eyes. Ceramide Complex CLR™ helps better moisturise demanding skin and fortify the skin barrier against free radicals and negative environmental influences.
Vitamin E, with its high antioxidant content, stimulates cell regeneration when signs of ageing appear. Fair trade apricot kernel oil maintains the natural protective layer of demanding skin. Together they unfold their smoothing properties and give the delicate eye area a fresh, silky appearance.
Revitalising eye cream with collagen
By replenishing the skin’s moisture depots, vegetable collagen, aloe vera and hyaluron promote the elasticity of the skin. Fair trade avocado oil and fine shea butter provide natural hydration. They protect the area around the eyes from dehydration. Bright eyes and a radiant appearance are desired side effects.

Usage
Apply gently around the eyes morning and evening after cleansing. Carefully apply from the inside out or pat in gently. Then admire your revitalised face in the mirror.

Product tips

Against puffy eyes: Store eye cream in the fridge! This increases the draining effect on eye bags and has a highly refreshing effect on tired eyes.
Extra care eye mask: 1x a week, prepare a pea-sized amount of eye cream for each eye area. Apply around the lower eye area, cover with moistened cotton pads and after approx. 15 minutes gently pat in any residue or carefully remove with the pads.
Eco-tip: Washable and reusable cotton pads do not lint, do not get caught in the eyelashes and are kind to the environment.
