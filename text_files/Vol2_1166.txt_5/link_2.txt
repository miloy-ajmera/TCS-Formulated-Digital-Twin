Crodamol™ PTIS

Technical Datasheet
|

Supplied by Croda







Pentaerythrityl Tetraisostearate. Crodamol™ PTIS by Croda is a rich, non-comedogenic emollient and water repellent imparting a soft cushiony feel to the skin. Exhibits low spreading capacity and high viscosity. It also functions as an excellent wetting agent for pigments and UV filters. It is a good alternative to castor oil and is preservative free. It imparts gloss, shine and vibrancy. Used in antiperspirants, deodorants, shaving, baby care, body care, facial care, sun care, eye products, facial make-up, lip products, creams and lotions. Also suitable for skin care, lip care, eye care, specific treatment-skin care, feet, hands, nails and sun protection. Other applications of Crodamol™ PTIS include after sun, skin protection, skin-male grooming, body-male grooming, lip-color cosmetics, face-color cosmetics and eye-color cosmetics.
