                        Crodamol™ PTIS
                    












Rich, non-comedogenic emollient imparting a soft cushiony feel to the skin. Low spreading capacity and high viscosity. Water repellent. Excellent wetting agent for pigments and UV filters. Good alternative to castor oil. Used in skin creams and lotions, sun care, colour cosmetics and bath oils.
INCI name
Pentaerythrityl Tetraisostearate
