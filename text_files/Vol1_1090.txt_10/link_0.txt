Description
Organic Ultra Rich Massage & Body Cream is a combination of Certified Organic and natural seed oils, that are rich in essential fatty acids and botanical extracts. This formula offers optimum skin nutrition, along with imparting a satin feel to one’s skin, and perfect glide for a wonderful massage experience. Feels like a cream, acts like an oil, once absorbed in skin moisturizes and does not feel greasy.
It is water dispersible, and contains no nut oils or harsh preservatives. Use Ultra Rich Massage & Body Cream either for spa or massage treatments, or as a wonderful body treatment cream. Our Ultra Rich Massage & Body Cream is one of our many premier massage products.
*Formerly known as Dual Purpose Massage and Body Cream.
Ingredients:
Aqueous (Deionized Water) infusion of botanical extracts of Calendula Officinalis, Aloe Barbadensis, Matricaria Recutita (Chamomile), Passiflora Incarnate, Arcitium lappa (Burdock), Custom Blend of Vitis Vinefera Oil (Grapeseed Oil), Rosa Mosqueta Oil (Rosehip Seed Oil), Aloe Barbadensis Oil, Linum Usitatissmum (Flax Seed Oil), Simmondsia Chinensis (Jojoba Oil), Prunus Armeniaca Kernel Oil, Borago Officinalis, Octyl Palmitate (Esther of Palm Oil), Cetyl Alcohol, Steryl Alcohol, Cetearyl Alcohol, Steareth-30, Ceteth-10, Tocopherol (Vitamin E), Essential oil perfume blend, Cabomer, Phenoxyethanol, Ethylhecylglycerin.
