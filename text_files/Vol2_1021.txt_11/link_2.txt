Barrier care just got better. This dermatologist & allergy-tested, fragrance-free, moisturizing face cream is amazing for sensitive skin. Formulated with vegan ceramides, amino acids and shea butter, this melt-into skin cream hydrates & helps restore your protective skin barrier. It helps restore the protective barrier function of the skin.
