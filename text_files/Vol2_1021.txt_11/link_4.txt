Sensitive & dry skin - meet your match! This dermatologist-tested, fragrance-free, moisturizing eye cream is formulated with vegan ceramides to support the skin barrier around your eyes. This lightweight formula works like a serum to replenish moisture under eyes, and marshmallow root extract and calendula help to de-puff. What makes our ceramides special?Our vegan blend is a plant-based lipid concentrate that your skin recognizes. It helps restore the protective barrier function of the skin.



        DETAILS 
        





Made for sensitive skin
Dermatologist & allergy tested
Fragrance-free formula
Formulated with vegan ceramides to support & replenish your skin barrier
Lightweight formula acts like a hydrating serum for under-eyes
Calendula & marshmallow root extract help soothe
Formulated without parabens, phthalates, SLS or mineral oil
