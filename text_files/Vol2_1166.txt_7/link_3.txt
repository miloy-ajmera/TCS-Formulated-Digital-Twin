Syncrowax™ BB4

Technical Datasheet
|

Supplied by Croda







Synthetic beeswax. Acts as emulsion stabiliser, glossing agent and rigidity agent. Ideal choice for lipsticks to stabilise color, enhance gloss and facilitate mold removal. Used in cosmetic sticks, skin care creams, lotions, salves, sun care, color cosmetics - eye, face, lips, hair care, lip care, eye care, specific treatment, treatments, relaxing/ permanent waves, sun protection, after-sun and skin protection.
