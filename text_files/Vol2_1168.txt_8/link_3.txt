Hostaphat® KL 340 D



Technical Datasheet
|

Supplied by Clariant










Trilaureth-4 Phosphate. Hostaphat® KL 340 D by Clariant is a cold-processable, pre-neutralized, anionic O/W emulsifier. It displays very good compatibility with alcohols, electrolytes, UV filters and all types of oils.
It is very well suitable for low viscosity formats and textures like thin, pumpable lotions, sprays & milks.
It offers pleasant skin feel, spreadability, quick absorption and lightweight after feel.
It is recommended for formulating color cosmetics, face-, body and hair care products.
It is also used in wet wipes, antiperspirants & deodorants, creams & lotions, hair styling and sun protection products.
Hostaphat® KL 340 D is Vegan suitable.
It has a shelf life of 24 months.
