Now with probiotics to help defend against age-accelerating environmental stressors, reduce inflammation, and the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.
A rich, intensive formula that features naturally hydrating and moisture retaining ingredients. It generously supplies the skin with nutrients, peptides and antioxidants, and acts as a "second skin" to reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles while smoothing the skin. The intensive formula continues to retain and deliver water even when it feels dry on the surface of the skin.









Benefits-Contains the HylaFusion®, a clinically proven breakthrough hyaluronic acid complex, which leaves skin feeling fresh,smooth and hydrated with no greasy residue.-Intensive hydration leaves skin supple and smooth-Reduces the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles-Soothing to irritated skin-Nourishing and anti-aging-Formulated with probiotics to help defend against age-accelerating environmental stressors, reduce inflammation, and the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.










How to UseAfter cleansing apply daily to the face, neck, and chest. For optimal results, use after the eraclea Intensive Repair and Protect Serum/Vitamin C.
