Description

Illuminate your eye area. Our unique multi-peptide complex helps to brighten and diminish the appearance of dark circles, while also improving skin tone and restoring firmness. This highly concentrated formula quickly melts into skin to reduce puffiness, smooth wrinkles, and deliver a wellspring of long-lasting hydration.

How to Use: Using your ring finger, gently pat a small amount onto the eye contour.
When to Use: Use daily, AM and PM, as the last step in your routine. For a bonus dose of hydration, close your eyes and spritz the EVE LOM Radiance Face Mist over your face.
