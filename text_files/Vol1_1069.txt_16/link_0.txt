Silky Mask is a soothing mask which can be applied to impure problem skin with acne, oily skin, and combination skin.
Silky Mask has an anti-inflammatory and normalizing effect, while selected vitamin compounds support cell formation. It refines the skin’s texture without drying it out and loosens clogged pores and hornifications.
The skin becomes clear and pure.
Silky Mask strengthens the skin’s natural defensive power against inflammation.
It is allergy- tested, and free from irritating substances.
 
Application:  According to skin condition, apply generously once or twice a week to cleansed face and neck areas (avoid eye area). The mask dries on the skin. After approximately 15-20 minutes, remove with moist pad and rinse with plenty of lukewarm water.  At night, it can be placed directly on the pimple.
Active ingredients: Biopollin, bolus alba, dragosantol, hamamelis, lanolin, purecellin, salicylic acid
