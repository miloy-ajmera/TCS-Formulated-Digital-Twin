Preparation:
        1. Mix Sequence #1 ingredients at 80?C with medium speed propeller stirring for 30 minutes.
2. Mix Sequence #2 ingredients and heat to 80°C.
3. Add Sequence #2 to Sequence #1 under propeller mixer. Mix for 15 minutes or until the
emulsification is completed.
4. Start cooling batch down with medium mixing.
5. Add Sequence #3 ingredient with mixing and continue cooling.
