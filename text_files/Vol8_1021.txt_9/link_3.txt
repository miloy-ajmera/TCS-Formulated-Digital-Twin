 NIGHTTIME PROTECTIVE OINTMENTS
REFRESH P.M.®
PRESERVATIVE-FREE LUBRICANT EYE OINTMENT




REFRESH P.M.® is a preservative-free ointment designed to relieve more intense burning, irritation, and discomfort due to eye dryness.It soothes your eyes while you sleep and protects them from nighttime dryness due to exposure. Ideal for use at bedtime.

Fact

                Certain over-the-counter and prescription medications can also be a cause of eye dryness.

Warnings






Mineral Oil (42.5%)Eye Lubricant


White Petrolatum (57.3%) Eye Lubricant






                            For the temporary relief of burning, irritation, and discomfort due to dryness of the eye or exposure to wind or sun.
                        

                            May be used as a protectant against further irritation.
                        





                            For external use only
                        

                            To avoid contamination, do not touch tip of container to any surface. Replace cap after using.
