MOIRA Rose Collagen Squalane Eye Cream is a lightweight, fast-absorbing cream to help improve skin elasticity and even out skin tone for a radiant complexion. Infused with Rose, Collagen, and Squalane, this formula will hydrate and restore your skin, keeping it smooth, youthful-looking, and plump. Gentle enough for daily use around the delicate eye area. For all skin types.

Cruelty-Free

Paraben Free
Sulfates Free
Phthalate Free
Made in Korea


HOW TO USE:
1. Apply in small dots around the eye area2. Gently smooth until thoroughly absorbed.

*When using this product, avoid getting into eyes. If contact occurs, rinse thoroughly with water.
