MIGLYOL®

Cosmetics ingredients supplied by IOI Oleo


MIGLYOL® by  IOI Oleo consists of a range of emollients that are available as 100% natural or vegan grades suitable for applications ranging from sun care to color cosmetics. Most of the grades are extremely light-weight and leave the skin with a velvety touch.
IOI Oleo offers grades that can be made customer-specific by offering:
Choice of polarityChoice of spreading behaviorChoice of sensorial profile
