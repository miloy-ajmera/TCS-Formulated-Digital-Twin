Product Overview


INCI Water (and) Glycerin (and) Hydrolyzed Corn ProteinFunctional botanical that helps skin build its natural barrier function and improve stratum corneum moisture and biomechanical properties. 





Product Specifications



Form: Yellow limpid solutionSolubility: Water solublePreservative system: Potassium Sorbate and Sodium BenzoateUse level: 1.0% (clinically tested at 1.0%)






Features & Benefits


Enhances expression of cornified envelope precursors, in-vitro and ex-vivoEnhances expression and enzymatic activity of all epidermal transglutaminases, in-vitroReinforces stratum corneum from stripping, in-vivoNature-derivedVegan suitable





Applications


Ideal for aftershave products and products used after skin peels or dermabrasion, given that these actions may disrupt the skin’snatural barrier function. also ideal for moisturizing formulations for dry, flaky sin and sensitive skin.
