What Is It?

Banila Co Dear Hydration Water Barrier Cream is an ultra-hydrating dermatologist tested formula is fast absorbing, and instantly reinvigorates, hydrates, and strengthens the skin. Pink Cactus Water provides a burst of healthy hydration, while Pink Biome™ preserves your skin’s natural barrier so you can glow all day (or all night) long!
 



How To Use:

Every morning and night, apply liberally onto face and neck in circular motions, keeping away from the eye & mouth area.

Benefits

Pink Cactus- also known as Prickly Pear, is rich in antioxidants & fatty acids to instantly target dry skin while helping replenish the skins moisture levels and leaving your skin with a natural glow.
