Product Overview


INCI Octyldodecyl StearateCeraphyl™ ODS is a branched emollient ester that can be used in lightweight and non-greasy feeling skin care formulas. In color cosmetics, it can be combined with Ceraphyl™ 791 ester and Emulsynt™ 1055, to efficiently bind high levels of pearls.





Product Specifications



Form: Colorless to pale yellow liquidAcid value: 3.0 maxSaponification value: 90 - 110Specific gravity @ 25°C: 0.843 - 0.863Refractive index @ 25°C: 1.4440 - 1.4640Use level: 2.0 - 20.0%
Primary Chemistry:  Octyldodecyl Stearate
