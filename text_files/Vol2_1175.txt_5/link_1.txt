Description


The Powerfully Rebuilding day & night cream is recommended for the daily care of 75+ skin, in need of an anti-wrinkle therapy. The formula, based on 24-carat colloidal gold, phenomenally irons out the skin and fills in even very deeply seated wrinkles, markedly reducing their length, width, and depth. A luxurious blend of black caviar and PepLIFT 4D firms, regenerates, and instantly lifts the skin. The extract of satsuma orange lightens all spots and discolorations apparent on the skin and evens out the skin tone. A unique combination of hyaluronic acid and vitamin C has superb moisturizing properties and rejuvenates the appearance of the skin. Ceramides rebuild the epidermal lipid barrier, intensively nourish, regenerate, and moisturize the skin, which is protected against photoaging by UVA/UVB filters.  24K COLLOIDAL GOLD phenomenally irons out the skin and fills in even very deeply seated wrinkles, markedly reducing their length, width, and depth. BLACK CAVIAR + PepLIFT 4D a blend of luxury and advanced technology for higher effectiveness. It firms, regenerates, and instantly lifts the skin. SATSUMA ORANGE lightens spots and discolorations apparent on the skin and evens out its tone. CERAMIDES rebuild the epidermal lipid barrier, intensively nourish, regenerate, and moisturize the skin.
