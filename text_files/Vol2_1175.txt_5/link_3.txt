Description


A rebuilding cream of a velvety formula which is readily absorbed, does not burden the skin, and makes applying make-up easier. Recommended for daily care of skin with visible symptoms of ageing. The cream guarantees intensive firming, supports repair processes in the skin, and rebuilds its protective barrier. Day by day, the skin becomes smoother and wrinkles – less apparent.  PEPTIDES improve skin elasticity and tension by maintaining optimum levels of collagen and hyaluronic acid in the skin. This permits to reduce the length and depth of wrinkles and makes the skin soft and velvety to the touch. CERAMIDES regenerate the skin’s lipid barrier, protecting it against the negative influence of external factors. They also nourish and intensively moisturise the skin, at the same time protecting it against transepidermal water loss. OILS OF YOUTH a complex of natural oils of powerful conditioning properties. It provides an intensive smoothing and moisturising effect, at the same time supporting regeneration and improving the skin’s barrier functions.  Usage description: Apply every morning and evening to cleansed skin on face and neck. For home use. Can be applied all year round.
