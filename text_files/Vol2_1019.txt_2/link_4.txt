Soraya Kolagen Elastyna Anti-Wrinkle Cream for Day and Night 50ml
Soraya Kolagen Elastyna Anti-Wrinkle Cream for Day and Night gently and effectively nourishes the skin, ensuring a clear smoothing of fine wrinkles. Intensively moisturizes and gently lubricates the skin, supporting its regeneration. It clearly improves the elasticity, firmness and smoothness of the face, neck and cleavage.
Active ingredients

The collagen formula visibly improves firmness and smoothness of the skin of face, neck and décolletage.
Elastin will make your skin regain suppleness, smoothness and become pleasant to the touch.
The cosmetic has been enriched with vitamins C, A and E provide effective care for the skin ensuring the visible smoothing of small wrinkles, speed up the regeneration of skin cells, improve the skin’s lipid barrier and protect it from dryness.

Directions
Apply every morning and evening to the cleansed skin of your face, neck and décolletage. Massage in gently. The cream gets absorbed quickly and works perfectly as a daily care, and also as a protective cream in winter.
