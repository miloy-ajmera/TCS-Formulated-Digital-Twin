DESCRIPTION
Cicapair Re-Cover Treatment is the all-in-one solution, this innovative formula contains a color-shifting capsule that bursts to match your skin tone as you apply it, as well as forms a dust shield to protect the skin from environmental stressors, boosting vitality.
A green-to-beige color-correcting treatment that corrects redness and protects skin from UV damage and environmental aggressors.
Redness and irritation disappear, as well as uneven skin tone. This treatment is also infused with sodium, potassium, calcium, and magnesium to help improve the skin’s moisture levels and restore fatigued skin, as well as resurrection plants houttuynia cordata and yarrow for an overall healthy appearance.
 
Free of artificial fragrance or color, denatured alcohol, triethanolamine, sulfate-based surfactants, triclosan, mineral oil, propylene glycol, and DEA that causes skin irritation.
HOW TO USE
After cleansing and toning, apply all over face and neck; cream will shift colors from green to beige to correct and protect your skin. Tip for dry skin types: Apply a layer of moisturizer before using this treatment.
