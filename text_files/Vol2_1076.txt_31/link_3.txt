Description



            Description
        

            Extract obtained from Equisetum Arvense L. (Horsetail) plants. Contains 20% extract dissolved in water and glycerin. Natural emulsifying and mattifying properties; creates a matte finish while maintaining good moisturizing features. Used for centuries as anti-irritant and soothing agent. Also used in deodorants.
        



            Use
        

            Recommended use level 5-10% in skin care products. For external use only.
        



            Applications
        

            All kinds of skin and hair care products. Also useful in makeup products.
