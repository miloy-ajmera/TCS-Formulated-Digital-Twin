Avuva
Vanilla Marshmallow Body Scrub



EGP 119.00
EGP 119.00







Avuva’s Vanilla Marshmallow Body Scrub doesn’t only brush away dead skin cells, dirt and skin pollutants with its rich creamy formula, it also deeply hydrates your skin. This body scrub is infused with panthenol, vitamin E and paraffin, which prevent water loss from your skin and keep your body feeling smooth and soft. Not only that, but it doesn’t leave greasy residue behind and promotes skin growth!

                  How To Use 




Whilst in the shower, apply a generous amount of the scrub to your body and massage gently all over for 10 minutes. Rinse off with warm water. Use 1-2 times per week for best results.








                  What's In It 




Water, cetyl alcohol, cetearyl alcohol, glyceryl monostearate, eumulgin B2, vaseline, paraffinum liquidum, glycerin, monopropylene glycol, d-panthenol, lithothamnion calcareum powder, polypropylene, vitamin E, phenoxyethanol.

200g
