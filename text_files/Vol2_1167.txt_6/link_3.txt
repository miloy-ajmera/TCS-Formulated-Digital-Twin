Syloid® FP Silica - Multifunctional Excipient for Pharmaceutical & Nutraceutical Formulations




















 
Grace Syloid® FP Silica offers efficiency and performance in many pharmaceutical and nutraceutical applications, helping to solve powder flow and caking challenges. Manufactured in the USA, Syloid FP silica is engineered hydrated silicon dioxide produced under controlled conditions, meeting USP/NF, EMA, Health Canada, and ANVISA requirements and offering high chemical purity and performance.
Univar Solutions can support your pharmaceutical excipient needs through a global network of credentialed distribution centers across North America, Europe, and Latin America. Our dedicated pharma ingredients team has the technical expertise to help you navigate the research, development, and commercialization phases of your pharmaceutical or nutraceutical product journey.
