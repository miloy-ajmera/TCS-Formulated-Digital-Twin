Special Moist is special moisturising care containing Collapur and additional factors.
It supplies the skin with moisture and increases the skin’s moisture-binding power.This hydro-active effect is supplemented by Carotene Oil and vitamin extracts, which prevent the hornification, flaking and tightening of the skin. 
It is therefore a necessity in case of moisture – lacking skin (dehydrated skin) as the skin gains tonicity and elasticity.
The light textured emulsion is quickly absorbed by the skin, and it is suitable for all skin type as basic care under the day or night preparations. Special Moist is an excellent make-up base, and as a first step under sun protection products.



Application: Apply on cleansed skin, and follow with day care, night care, make-up or sun protection.
 
Main ingredients: Allantoin, Biocorno, Carotene, Collapur, Cutavit Complex, Karion F
