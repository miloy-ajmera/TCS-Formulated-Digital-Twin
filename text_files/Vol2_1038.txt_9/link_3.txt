Escalol 577 is a UV filter that offers protection against UV-A and UV-B radiation. Escalol™ 577 can be used in sunscreens, body and facial care formulas and colour cosmetics to deliver SPF protection.
Features & Benefits of Escalol 577

Secondary UVB absorber with activity in the UVA region
Relatively water soluble
Recommended at low levels (<0.5%) as a colour protestant for products in clear packages
Approved use level: up to 5.0% (E.U.) and up to 10.0% (U.S.)

Sunscreens
Body & Facial Care
Colour Cosmetics
