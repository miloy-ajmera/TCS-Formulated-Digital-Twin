Cromoist™ O-25

Technical Datasheet
|

Supplied by Croda







Hydrolyzed Oats (and) Aqua. Cromoist™ O-25 by Croda acts as conditioning and moisturizing agent. It imparts soft skin feel. It has hair softening benefits. Cromoist™ O-25 is used in moisturising skin creams and lotions, detergent cleansers, including liquid soaps and shower gels, conditioning shampoos, leave-on/rinse-off conditioners, sun care products and styling products.
