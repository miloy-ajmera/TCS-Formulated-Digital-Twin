Description
Emulgade 1000 NI is a non-ionic, self-emulsifying base with consistency giving properties, and is suited for the preparation of cosmetic oil-in-water lotions and creams. It is a white product with a faint characteristic odor, which is supplied in pellets. This product has an acid value of max. 0.5, a hydroxyl value of 175-190, a saponification value of max. 1, and a pH value (1%) of 6-8.
