MIGLYOL® 840

Technical Datasheet
|

Supplied by IOI Oleo







Caprylic/Capric Triglyceride. MIGLYOL® 840 by IOI Oleo acts as an light and dry emollient. It offers excellent spreadability on the skin and good skin absorption. It shows xcellent penetration-promoting and skin-smoothing properties. It provides good solubility characteristics and do not inhibit skin-respiration. MIGLYOL® 840 is used in day creams, body lotions, sun protection products, foundations, lip sticks, baby care and wet wipe cleansing lotions.
