Product Overview


INCI C12-15 Alkyl LactateCeraphyl™ 41 provides non-oily lubricity to hydro-alcoholic products. It is easy to emulsify and best at neutral pH.





Product Specifications



Form: Clear, water white to straw-colored liquidAcid value: 2.0 maxSaponification value: 195.0 - 210.0Specific gravity @ 25°C: 0.900 - 0.920Refractive index @ 25°C: 1.4430 - 1.4450Use level: 5.0 - 10.0%






Features & Benefits


Effective de-tackifying agent; reduces tackiness and greasinessEffectively solubilizes organic sunscreen actives Oxyzenzone (Benzophenone-3) and Avobenzone (Butyl Methoxydibenzoylmethane)Spreads easily when appliedDry initial feel with non-oily after-feelBuilds viscosity in shampoos and body washesProvides lather creaminess in shampoos and body washesThickenerReplaces DEA in surfactant systemsBased on alpha hydroxy acidsEasy to emulsifyCompatible with many systemsBest in pH-neutral formulationsBiodegradable31% natural origin (lactic acid)Vegan suitable
