Crodalan™ AWS

Technical Datasheet
|

Supplied by Croda







Polysorbate 80 (and) Cetyl Acetate (and) Stearyl Acetate (and) Oleyl Acetate (and) Acetylated Lanolin Alcohol. Crodalan™ AWS by Croda acts as an emollient, superfatting agent and emulsifier. Suitable for aqueous alcoholic systems, bath oils and aerosol antiperspirants. Also used in skin care creams, lotions, sun care, skin toners, astringents, hair shampoos and conditioners. Other applications of Crodalan™ AWS include aqueous alcoholic systems, styling products, color cosmetics, aftershaves, colognes and antiperspirants.
